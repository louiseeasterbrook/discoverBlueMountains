Leura

Sublime Point Lookout

Sublime Point is the most popular lookout in Leura that allows visitors to peer into the Jamison valley within the Blue Mountains National park. The most notable feature of the lookout is Mt Solitary.

This viewing spot is located next to the popular rock climbs ‘Sweet Dreams’ and ‘Hells Bells’ and is also a popular spot for bird watchers to spot some native Australian birds in their natural habitat. These attractions make the lookout a popular hotspot for activities.

From the car park to the lookout it is a 200m walk with some rocky areas. It is not recommended for people who are not stable on their feet.

Distance:200m from carpark
Parking: Car park available
Toilet:Not available

Leura Mall

Leura Mall is the main street of Leura. The street is filled with a variety of interesting stores ranging from cafes, tourist shops, hobby shops and clothing stores (Many of the stores are locally owned businesses). This is the perfect place to pick up a Blue Mountains souvenir.

During Spring the street is alive and blooming with bright Australian Flora. This street is a popular morning spot to have brunch and is the perfect stopping point when heading to suburbs further up the mountain.

Be aware that most stores close during the early to mid-afternoon on weekends.

Parking: Car park available
Toilet: Available
